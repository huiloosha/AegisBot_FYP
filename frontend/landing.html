Why AegisBot?

AegisBot is an intelligent cyber hygiene assessment platform designed to help individuals understand and improve their cybersecurity practices. By combining behavioural assessment, password strength analysis, and machine learning, the system estimates the user’s cyber risk level and provides actionable recommendations tailored to their security habits.

  • Takes less than 5 minutes
  • Evidence-based assessment
  • Personalized recommendations

How It Works

Behavior Assessment

Evaluate your daily cybersecurity habits using research-based questions designed to identify common security risks and unsafe online behaviours.

Step 1

Password Strength Analysis

Assess the strength of your password by analysing its complexity, length, and overall security without permanently storing your password.

Step 2

Machine Learning Risk Prediction

Generate a personalized cyber hygiene risk score using a machine learning model trained to identify behavioural patterns associated with cybersecurity risk.

Step 3

Progress Tracking

Monitor your assessment history, observe improvements over time, and continuously strengthen your cybersecurity practices.

Step 4

Predictive Cyber-Risk Personal Assistant Through Behavior-Based Analytics Using Machine Learning
