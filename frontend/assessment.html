Purpose

This tool assesses your cyber hygiene through behavioural questions and password analysis to estimate your cyber risk level and provide recommendations.

Password Privacy

Your password is analysed only to evaluate its strength. It is never stored, logged, or used for authentication.

Data Usage

If you are logged in, your assessment results are saved to your account so you can track your progress over time. Guest assessments are not stored.

Educational Purpose

This system is developed as a Final Year Project for educational and research purposes and does not replace professional security advice.
